    About

    This book reports early results in the first update of the Strengthening the Reporting of Empirical Simulation Studies (STRESS) guidelines for Discrete-Event Simulation, Agent Based Simulation and System Dynamics. Results should be viewed as draft and subject to change.

    Monks, T., Currie, C. S. M., Onggo, B. S., Robinson, S., Kunc, M., & Taylor, S. J. E. (2018). Strengthening the reporting of empirical simulation studies: Introducing the STRESS guidelines. Journal of Simulation, 13(1), 55–67. https://doi.org/10.1080/17477778.2018.1442155
